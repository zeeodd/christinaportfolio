About

In April 2019, I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics at Claremont Graduate University. Under the supervision of Dr. Ami Radunskaya and Dr. Johanna Hardin from Pomona College, I investigated the distribution of the betweenness centrality in exponential random graph models. If you would like to know more about my background or work, check out my CV or publications.
